# Stroke-Prediction-Project
- Contributed to project that looked at wheter health and lifestyle factors could predict the chance someone would have a stroke
- Looked at factors such as age, gender, smoking, and marital status
- Foctors were used to test which most heavily contributed to the likeliness of a stroke occuring
- Findings included: factors pertaining to physical health were the ones that affected the likelihood of someone having a stroke. Type of employment and marriage status were non-physical factors that had the highest affects on the model
